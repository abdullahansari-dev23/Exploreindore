The Memorable Journey of Indore

ORIGIN

Birth of Indore

Indore originated as a modest trading settlement due to its strategic position on major trade routes. Over time, merchants, artisans, and travelers transformed it into a thriving marketplace.

1747
ROYAL ERA

Holkar Dynasty

Under the Holkar rulers, Indore flourished as a royal capital. Architectural masterpieces like Rajwada Palace and Lal Bagh Palace reflected political strength, culture, and refined artistry.

1818
GROWTH

Post-Independence Expansion

After independence, Indore rapidly evolved into a major educational, industrial, and commercial center, becoming the backbone of Madhya Pradesh’s economic growth.

1956
PRIDE

India’s Cleanest City

Indore redefined urban cleanliness by consistently ranking as India’s cleanest city — driven by disciplined waste management, strong governance and active citizen participation.

2017
TODAY

Smart & Cultural Capital

Today, Indore stands as a Smart City — blending technology, metro connectivity, world-famous food culture, education, and a vibrant lifestyle while proudly preserving its historic soul.

2024
